What is Fruits

In botany, fruit is the seed-bearing structure in flowering plants formed from the ovary after flowering. Fruits are the means by which angiosperms disseminate seeds.

List Of Fruits

  • Apple
  • Mango
  • Banana
  • Grapes

Uses Of Fruits

  1. Fruits protect seeds
  2. They ripen as seeds mature
  3. Then they help disburse seeds rather than protect them
  4. Fruits help plants spread seeds out
